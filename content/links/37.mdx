> Element rolls off the beaten path to discover Earth’s most scenic skate spots.

After the [last link](/links/36) the algorithm served me up another new skateboard vid. So many locations, really transports you. I have no idea where they were... France? Spain? South America? Scandinavia? Manchester? Maybe all of them.

Really artfully done and something so pure about a group of people having a good time whilst making something wonderful.

That old pipe in the forest looked wild, when I was 23 I did a long road trip with a bunch of Swedes all the way up to Umeå to shoot in a similar looking location. Really bought back memories.
