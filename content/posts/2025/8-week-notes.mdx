## I’m a zwiftee now

I recently borrowed a friend’s turbo trainer and I’m six weeks into giving Zwift a go. I wouldn’t call it enjoyable but it doing wonders for my fitness. Soon the mornings will be light (and warm) enough to resume my 6am rides but in the meantime it’s really satisfying to know I’m getting fitter.

## gif.land

I’ve still been tinkering a lot with [gif.land](https://gif.land), I’m finding it really rewarding. I’ve not used Cloudflare’s APIs much before and it’s all so easy to use and well documented.

I’ve added [info pages](https://home.gif.land/info/bravery.gif) for each gif — I mainly did it just so I could make them go full screen which is silly but fun. I even got to use [CSS blend mode stuff](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/mix-blend-mode) which is fun in a _it’s 2004 and I’ve just discovered photoshop blend modes_.
