## Weekend in Aberporth

This weekend we went to Wales to catch up with friends but mainly to meet Jack and Holly’s three week old baby. I haven’t seen a baby that small in a while, they are so smol. Was great to meet them and see how well our friends were doing.

![Morning wave in Aberporth](https://imagedelivery.net/tfgleCjJafHVtd2F4ngDnQ/L1001337-Web/medium "caption")

We stayed in Aberporth at James and Siân’s house which is right on the beach and we got up to swim each morning. On Saturday the local fishing boat came in whilst we were there and the fisherman gave our friends a huge lobster.

The catch was mainly crab they land it and they drive it up to the main road where it’s loaded straight into a lorry with a tank in and then it’s driven to Spain, sad there’s no market for it in the UK.

It was 29°C over there, a welcome break from the temps here in London. The sea breeze helped and it was much cooler in the evening. We slept a lot better for it which was a relief after a few intolerable weeks in London.

On the Saturday it was a Jake’s birthday and him and Saz organised a little garden get-together which was a fun and made it really convenient to catch up with everyone at once.
