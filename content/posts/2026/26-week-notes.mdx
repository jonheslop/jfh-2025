Week two of the mega pan Europe heatwave, it started off still fairly novel and I felt we were just about keeping on top of things, with lots of shades, strategic opening/closing of windows, use of fans and using the portable aircon unit as a backstop.

But my Wednesday it was getting pretty unbareable, the fabric of our house was so thoroughly warmed that we couldn’t really get things much lower than 29ºC before midnight. Wednesday, Thursday and Friday we slept with the aircon on all night (and wore earplugs to make it bearable), luckily our sash windows work pretty well with it
