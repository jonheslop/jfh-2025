Started off the week by heading to Kent for our annual offsite with everyone from work. We were at [Bore Place](https://www.boreplace.org/), a fancy old house with a farm attached that makes cheese, it’s now run as a trust. The showers continued but there were lots of nice gaps with sunshine. They have a resident bat population and a bat detector thing we could borrow. We watched some pipistrelle’s come out at sunset. I had my binoculars so we could see them emgering from under the tiles. I hosted some social stuff over the 3 days, it all went well... it was nice to see everyone IRL.

World Cup’s on which means [Guessi](https://gues.si/) is back, they certainly left it to the last minute but it’s fun to play. I’ve got a 44% accuracy rate so far, terrible... a relief I’m not into gambling.
I’ve guessed Japan will beat Netherlands which we’ll soon find out about, still nil-nil at half time.

Met some friends Friday night at _The Dove_ on Broadway Market, was good to catch up. I had cycled down on my cargo bike and as Ian also lives in Walthamstow, I gave him a lift back which was fun extension of our chat. Much better than a getting a cab.

Spent a good few hours today and yesterday down the allotment, everything really popping now, we harvested our first cucumber! Bec spotted a frog in the pond, which is the first time since we put it in four years ago we’ve seen one. Hopefully it eats some slugs.

Still waiting for our mortgage on the new place to be finalised, every week we hope it’s happens and something delays it. Frustrating but hopefully we’re edging closer. We had a laser survey this week which means we’ll have drawings soon so that we can get started getting on the planning applications.

We watched _The Drama_ last night, good premise and I think it started really strong but the last act sorta didn’t quite land for me. Still, I’m a RPatz fanboi and Zendaya was brill also Alana Haim did a good job. [I gave it ★★★](https://letterboxd.com/jonheslop/film/the-drama/).
