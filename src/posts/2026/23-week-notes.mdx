Quiet week, weather has been stormy. Seen some good lightening. The plants at the allotment are loving it (including the weeds). 

Work moved office this week. We’ve been in Notting Hill on the top floor of an old town house. It was very startup like, no aircon, no showers, terrible kitchen. Now we’ve moved to a private office within the huge Victoria WeWork. I’d never been to one before. It’s a lot more comfortable for sure — all the mod cons. I didn’t really investigate much but plan to try going more often. 

Victoria itself is easier to get to but it’s a part of London I’ve never really enjoyed going to. Mostly reminds me of the coach station which I used a lot as a teenager.
