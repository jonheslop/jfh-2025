## Biome

I’d not heard of [Biome](https://biomejs.dev/), but [Phil mentioned it](https://www.gyford.com/phil/writing/2026/04/19/weeknotes/#s5). So I gave it a whirl, it really is very fast! Which is pretty meaningless on this small project. But for our work monorepo — the Prettier format is well slow (like 45s) and I tried it out with Biome and was under 2s, crazy! I’m not yet willing to enforce the change on my coworkers but I might do if I can get the config to make minimal difference to how things are now, I’d definitely switch.

## Eating out

Last week was quite unusual, insofar as we ended up eating out with friends three (3) times in one week, which felt like a treat.

On Wednesday, we went to [Impala](https://impalasoho.com/) with Jacob and Zoë, it was very much like [Mountain](https://mountainbeakstreet.com/), in a good way. Such a good vibe in there and of course the food was very good. The thing I notice the most about high-end restaurants is how polite and numerous the staff are, it makes you feel very cared for.

Then on Saturday we went for an early dinner at [The Tamil Crown](https://www.thetamilcrown.com/), we’d been to [The Tamil Prince](https://www.thetamilprince.com/) a few years back. I think I enjoyed this experience more, very good food and nice atmosphere. We met Jess and Jim in Haggerston and walked along the canal to get there which was nice start too. Afterwards, I begged everyone to indulge me in going to The Wenlock Arms, my favourite, they had a DJ playing records which was fun, although it didn’t sound great. As the night went on, it started to sound better, but I think that was just because of how many drinks I’d had 🙈

On Sunday we went to the newly opened [Bar Etna](https://maps.app.goo.gl/mQsDiL5a3m9JnyLp7) with Kate and Lianne for lunch. Really good vibe in there too and the pizza was delicious and pretty reasonably priced considering it felt quite primo. They had Lambrusco by the glass which as a big fan, I enjoyed. It was a sunny afternoon and we rode our bikes over and it’s times like that where I really feel lucky to live in London, just seeing everyone going about their day in the sunshine, you can’t beat it!

## Allotment update

There was a frost last Monday and it damaged (killed off all the leaves) on all our potatoes, which had only just come up, which was a bummer. Most of our seedlings are still in the greenhouse and are coming along well. There was a seedling swap on Saturday which was nice and we got quite a lot of things to bolster up crop. It’s going to be busy down there come the summer, hopefully lots of tomatoes and beans.

We’re still enjoying the asparagus, I did a risotto with some last night.
