## Garden wildlife

This week I was hanging washing out in the garden (yay for spring sunshine!) and Maude was pottering around. After a while she got fixated on one corver of our garden sniffing. As I got closer I realised I could hear muffled squeaking and so I looked over the top of the fence, and there was a fox (vixen) nursing a bunch of tiny kits! Very cute, I felt bad for disturbing them. The next evening Maude was asleep on the sofa near the window and I was on my computer, we both heard a tap on the window and looked out and the fox was there, paws on window sill just looking at us. Very weird, obviously Maude went mental and it ran off.

## House stuff

We’ve been keeping ourselves busy with DIY. About 18 months ago we had our loft converted but left it unfinished because we ran out of time/money before moving to Wales. So we’ve had builders in doing work but also been doing quite a lot ourselves. It was feeling quite a slog/overwhelming but we’re almost done now. 

Last weekend I finished the skirting, added some nice oak window sills and boarded off the eaves storage and added some cupboard doors. Hinges are hard. It looks great up there, it catches the light longer being higher up and it’s nice in the late afternoon.
